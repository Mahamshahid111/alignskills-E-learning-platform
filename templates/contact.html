Frequently Asked Questions

What is AlignSkills?

AlignSkills is an e-learning platform designed to provide high-quality courses and resources for skill development. You can learn at your own pace and earn certifications.

How do I register?

Simply click on the 'Login button' at the top-right corner of the page,then the login page will be appear now click the 'sign up button' on the right and then enter your details, and start exploring courses!

Are the courses free?

Yes! All courses on AlignSkills are completely free. Our platform is designed to offer high-quality educational resources without any hidden fees.

Can I earn a certificate?

Yes, once you complete a course, you will receive a certificate via email that you can add to your resume or share with potential employers.

How does the Resume Builder work?

The Resume Builder is completely free to use. You can input your personal information, skills, education, and experience, and download a professional resume template.

How can I contact support?

You can reach our support team via the 'Contact Us' section on our website. We are available 24/7 to assist you.
